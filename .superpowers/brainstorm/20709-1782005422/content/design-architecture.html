➔
Google ML Kit Text Recognition: Detects Japanese text blocks, extracts text string and precise bounding box coordinates.

➔
Quantized Gemma 4 (MediaPipe): Processes Japanese strings on-device. Generates English translations.

➔
Custom Window Overlay: Draws matching solid background color over original text, then renders English translation on top in matching bounds.
