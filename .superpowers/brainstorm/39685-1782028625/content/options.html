Layout & Gesture Design for Dismissing Translation Layer

Select the preferred design option for the close button and swipe-to-dismiss interaction.

Floating Close FAB (Bottom-Right) + Swipe Up Dismiss

A simple circular Close Button (FAB) in the bottom-right corner. Swiping up anywhere on the screen also dismisses the layer. Clean and unobtrusive.

B

Bottom Dismiss Bar (Close Button + Swipe Handle Indicator)

A semi-transparent bottom bar featuring a clear "Close" action in the bottom-right, alongside a centralized arrow/drag-handle visual indicating the swipe-up gesture is supported. High discoverability.

C

Minimal Floating Chip (Bottom-Right) + Dual-Swipe Dismiss

A small pill-shaped chip containing "Close ×" in the bottom-right. Dismisses on either swipe up or swipe down gesture, offering maximum flexibility.
